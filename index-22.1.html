Mobile Applications & Business Strategy

Our Mobile Applications & Business Strategy offering is focused on helping businesses establish and execute a consumer mobility strategy. Our approach focuses on three core areas:

 

1. Defining the overall strategic direction for mobility, identify & formalize vision and guiding principles for consumer-facing mobility program

 

2. Describing target-state functionality as well as process & technology capabilities required to achieve target state

 

3. Developing prioritization framework & deliver roadmap for features / capabilities

Enterprise Mobility Strategy & Consulting

Our Enterprise Mobility Strategy & Consulting offering helps clients to lay the foundation for a successful mobility program. These can be targeted at customers, employees, partners or all of the above. We typically approach these projects in three phases:

1. Assessment: Review current state and perform benchmarking. In the assessment phase we use a combination of interview, survey's and/or workshops.

2. Blueprint: Define mobile strategy and capabilities. In the blueprint phase we examine the possible solutions and test our hypotheses with the clients to create our blueprint recommendations.

3. Roadmap: Define implementation roadmap. The final phase is the roadmap phase where we prioritize the recommendations and plot them out over time. We also create the executive summary and socialize the findings in this phase.

API Strategy & Consulting

The API Strategy & Consulting offerings are tailored based on the needs of the client. The three main consulting services are:

1. API Strategy and Roadmap: at the end of the project, we would have developed a clear objective, strategy, and roadmap for using APIs as an enabler of digital services.

2. API Enablement and Assessment: provides recommendations to align the API Program with the overall business strategy and objectives, such as defining the proper KPIs and metrics to measure the success of the API program, and identifying key roles and responsibilities that have been neglected.

3. Technology Blueprint & Proof of Concept (POC): creates a technology blueprint or provides support on the technical architecture or design of the API platform, this service can also be provided in conjunction with consultants from E-TECH's Advanced Technology Architecture group.
